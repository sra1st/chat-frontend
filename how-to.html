how to bunny

✦ how to bunny ✦

a tiny moonlit guide for hopping into bunnychat, sharing a secret code, and keeping your messages cozy in the garden.
1 meet your bunny self

Pick a name that feels like soft paws and glitter.

When you enter the chat, choose the name you want your bunny friends to see. It can be sweet, silly, mysterious, or all three.

2 share the secret code

Use the same bunny code as your favorite rabbit.

Everyone who wants in must type the same code. Think of it like a hidden burrow door. Same code = same garden.

the hop-by-hop journey

🐰
Hop in Type your name and bunny code, then press enter. You will land in the chat with a little “you hopped in” sparkle.
💬
Send a message Write in the message box and send a thought, a hello, a carrot joke, or a secret from the moonlit garden.
↩
Reply to a message Tap the reply button on any bubble to answer a specific bunny. Your reply will nest under the original message like a warm little burrow.
✦
Watch the magic You may see typing, read notes, reactions, and little starry system messages floating through the garden while everyone chats.
🚪
Hop off when you are done Use the exit door when it is time to leave. That clears your trail and ends the session like a bunny slipping back into the grass.
